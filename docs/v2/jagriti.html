Editor Guidelines

Editorial Standards
  • Review all submissions for originality and quality
  • Ensure content is appropriate for a community family magazine
  • Respect both English and Bengali (বাংলা) submissions equally
  • Provide constructive feedback for revisions — never harsh or personal
  • Use only the provided email templates for communication
  • All emails must go through the AI compliance check before sending
  • Flag inappropriate or offensive content to the President immediately
  • Maintain equal representation across categories when possible
  • Encourage youth and children's submissions

Important

Editors must use only the fixed email templates provided. Custom emails are not allowed. The AI compliance agent monitors all editorial communications for tone, purpose, and compliance with BANF's community standards.

Offensive Content Protocol

If any submission contains offensive, hateful, or inappropriate content, immediately flag it — the system will notify the President (Dr. Ranadhir Ghosh) automatically. Do not engage with the submitter about offensive content directly.
